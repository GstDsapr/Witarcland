# 维塔克兰 Witarcland

维塔克兰是一个包含科技与魔法的多模组魔改整合包。

Witarcland is a massive modified modpack that integrates technology and magic.

在维塔克兰中，多个模组的发展线相互交织，玩家很难长时间致力于单一模组的发展。此外，维塔克兰被划分为五个主要阶段，并以此分出了五个章节的任务，每一章的任务通常以进入特定的维度结束。

In Witarcland, the development lines of multiple mods are intertwined, making it difficult for players to focus on the development of a single mod for an extended period. Additionally, Witarcland is divided into five main stages, which further correspond to five chapters of quests. Each chapter of quests typically ends with entering a specific dimension.

维塔克兰还包含一些有趣的模块化机械，例如使启命英变的更简单的生命游戏、包含有中国八卦元素的世界祭坛。还有诸如一旦进入就难以离开的核泄漏之地、永远无法出现在玩家身上的凝聚不稳定等等设定。

Witarcland also features some interesting Modular Machinery, such as the Game of Life that simplifies Dandelifeon, and the World Altar incorporating elements of Chinese Bagua. Furthermore, it includes unique settings like the Wasteland that is hard to leave once entered, and Condensed Unstable which can never appear on players.

总之，维塔克兰不是一个单纯通过魔改合成表来使游戏变复杂的整合包，更多的内容希望你可以在游戏中体会到。

In short, Witarcland is not a modified modpack that merely complicates the game by modifying crafting recipes. We hope you can experience more of its content in the game.
